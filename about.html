📖 About Image Converter Pro

✨ What is Image Converter Pro?

A powerful, privacy-focused image conversion tool that runs entirely in your browser. No uploads, no servers, no tracking — just fast, secure image processing on your device.

⚡ Features

  • Convert between JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats
  • Resize images with aspect ratio lock and presets
  • Quality control for lossy formats (JPEG, WebP)
  • Dark mode for comfortable night use
  • Before/after comparison slider
  • 100% local processing - images never leave your device

🔒 Privacy Guarantee

Zero data leaves your device. All image processing happens locally using HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript. Your images are never uploaded to any server, making this tool safe for sensitive photos, documents, or personal images.

📱 Browser Support

Works on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and modern mobile browsers.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Use WebP for the smallest file sizes
  • PNG is best for images with transparency (logos, icons)
  • JPEG at 85% quality is the sweet spot for photos
